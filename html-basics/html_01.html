Introduction

To travel is fun, we get to visit unknown places which are special in their own way. But travelling can be cumbersome and monotonous if not planned properly. So, here is my blog which has tips on how to travel, essential items to be packed, and how smartly to do it so that you can make your trip memorable.
    Travel with less baggage
    Wear comfortable clothing
    Watch out for the weather at your destination
    Make sure you have hard cash/ currency
    Carry lightweight luggage but strong one

Travel Modes and Tips

Travel modes as we all know can only be by Land, by Air and by Water

By Air

Air travel modes are either Hot Air Baloon, Plane, biplane etc. Durable check in luggage with flashlight, clothes, carry on which should be light and include medicines, notebook, a pen, electronic device like IPad, Tablet, Laptop, computer, brush, paste and a book to read to spend your time in air.

By Water

Water bottle, water proof light clothing, change of dress, water bottle, ziplocks, quick snacks, compass and most important of all - 'Never Travel Alone'

By Land

Definitely the most preferred way to travel since you can enjoy long drives and switch between drivers if you are in your own 4 wheeler, Good Travel shoes, durable cloting, water, medicine, map, driving directions, trash bags, quick snacks and yes relaible transportation and a good travel partner.
